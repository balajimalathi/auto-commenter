# Target Guide for X/Twitter

> When writing X/Twitter replies, reflect each target's characteristics.

---

## Target List

| Target          | Followers/Posts | Daily Limit |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| For you         | ~500K posts | 25 |
| Following       | ~2M posts | 25 |
| Build in Public | ~300K posts | 25 |
| Fail in Public  | ~50K posts | 25 |
| Smol            | ~1M followers | 25 |

---

## Target-Specific Guides

### For You

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Community Nature** | - Algorithm-curated mix of trending topics<br>- Broad interests based on your follows/engagement<br>- High-signal posts from diverse creators |
| **Good Topics to Engage** | - Viral threads with genuine discussion<br>- Fresh takes on hot topics<br>- Questions seeking advice/opinions |
| **Tone** | Curious, conversational, value-adding |

***

### Following

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Community Nature** | - People/brands you already follow<br>- Established relationships/expectations<br>- Higher trust threshold |
| **Good Topics to Engage** | - Updates from consistent creators<br>- Questions about their recent work<br>- Their calls for feedback/collaboration |
| **Tone** | Familiar, supportive, relationship-building |

***

### Build in Public

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Community Nature** | - Indie hackers shipping publicly<br>- Transparent progress updates<br>- Founder journey documentation |
| **Good Topics to Engage** | - Launch day experiences<br>- User acquisition struggles<br>- Revenue milestones<br>- Tech stack decisions<br>- Pivot stories |
| **Tone** | Encouraging, battle-tested, founder empathy |

***

### Fail in Public

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Community Nature** | - Founders sharing failures/mistakes<br>- Learning from setbacks<br>- Vulnerability in business |
| **Good Topics to Engage** | - Product flops and lessons<br>- Wrong assumptions validated<br>- Burnout/recovery stories<br>- Bad hires/customer churn |
| **Tone** | Empathetic, battle-scarred, resilient |

***

### Smol

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Community Nature** | - Curated high-signal accounts<br>- Quality over quantity<br>- Your personal "watch list" |
| **Good Topics to Engage** | - Updates from key influencers<br>- Their questions/requests<br>- Content you want to amplify<br>- Collaboration opportunities |
| **Tone** | Respectful, thoughtful, relationship-focused |
